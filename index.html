01The ritual

Cloudy ice melts fast, waters down your cup, and quietly ruins a brew you worked to get right.

The problem. You dial in the grind, weigh the water, nail the bloom — then drop in a cube of freezer ice that dilutes it in minutes. All that care, gone by the second sip.

The ritual. SAFA freezes slow and clear, so ice melts at the pace of a proper drink. The recipe you brewed is the recipe you taste — first sip to last.

03How it works

Freeze clear.
Reuse naturally.

Step 01

Freeze top-down

An insulated cork body forces ice to grow slowly from the top, pushing every cloudy bubble downward and out of the cube.

Step 02

Drain & reuse

The cloudy water underneath never freezes — pull the plug and it drains out as liquid, ready to pour back in.

Saves 30–50% water
Step 03

Enjoy 4 crystal-clear columns

A freeze-line window tells you exactly when they’re done. Flex the mold and four clear columns drop free — two make one perfect iced V60.
